About RT4Coins

Project Mission

RT4Coins hopes to inform the readers about the opaque and fast growing world of cryptocurrency giveaways on Twitter. In December 2019, Coinkit and Cctip, the first two bots in this space, started offering their tipping services to users. Since then, the volume and value of tips has skyrocketed, surpassing 1.000.000 tips in March 2020. The goal of this website is to educate people about how these Twitter bots work and where to find information about them.

The project was created in Copenhagen, Denmark by Maxwell Burda during a web development class at the IT University Copenhagen.

Picture of the IT University Copenhagen by Henning Larsen

Project Lead

Maxwell Burda

Currently, Maxwell is a student as the Copenhagen Business School, studying an M.Sc. program in E-Business. He is passionate about work at the intersection of business and technology, spending a lot of his time reading about product and software development.

In December 2019, he stumbled upon several twitter accounts promoting giveaways that were being paid out via a bot. After reading through the comments, he found out that bots had been created to tip other users via twitter posts. In an effort to share his findings with the public, he created the webiste RT4Coins.com.
